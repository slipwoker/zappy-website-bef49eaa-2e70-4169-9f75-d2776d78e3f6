Make changes, see results

Object properties

Adjust mass, velocity, spring constants, and more in real time. Change m=2 kg to m=5 kg and watch gravity's effect shift instantly. The inspector panel gives you direct control over every physical parameter in your scene.

Constraints & connections

Link objects with springs, ropes, or fixed joints to model complex systems. Set k=50 N/m for a soft spring or increase stiffness to simulate rigid connections. The constraints panel helps you build everything from pendulums to pulley systems.

Run and reflect

Hit play to watch your simulation unfold, then pause to analyze. Track velocity like v=3 m/s in real time or step through frame by frame. The timeline controls let you explore cause and effect at your own pace, building intuition for how physics actually works.
